Magnovite is the annual fest scheduled for 26-27 March 2021. Collectively, Magnovite has 5 Technical events, 9 Management events, 4 Architecture events, 4 Psychology events and 4 Cultural events. Magnovite aims to be a multi-disciplinary approach to innovation, achieved through a collaborative effort of the best minds from different disciplines. In Magnovite, we curate a fest with fusion of technology and culture to make that ambition a reality.
