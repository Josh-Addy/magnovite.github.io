  • RC Aero Modelling

    Ever felt the need to fly higher and faster than everyone around you? Have you ever felt that you can surf the winds like no other? Then RC aeromodelling is the event for you. RC Aeromodelling is more than just engineering, it is where design meets functionality.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1.A team should comprise of a maximum of 5 members.
    2.A team should use only one model throughout the competition.
    3.Members of team may be from same or different college/school/clubs.
    4.Models should be built from scratch and should not be purchased.
    5.Only electrical motors are allowed.
    6.Professionals and professional clubs are restricted from participation.
    7.If anyone found not following the above rules, they will be disqualified.
    8.The team will have to submit a video, which would need to comply with the following criteria: -

    8.1 Each team member should be introduced in the video and should be wearing their college id cards (I.E we should be able to see your id card clearly).
    8.2 Model presentation by a team member or can also be a group presentation.
    8.3 A picture in picture format video for the take-off acrobatics and landing. (sample video will be shared)
    8.4The team should have two cameras (can be that of a smartphone or a professional camera whichever is available to the team) for recording the acrobatic part with one camera fixated on the pilot form the start till the end of acrobatics and the other should be a wide angle shot with both the pilot and the drone (in the beginning) in the picture, which during flight of drone will shifts entirely to the drone
    8.5The pilot should confirm the fact that he is in fact controlling the same aero model via the controller following which he/she can proceed to the acrobatics. Also do confirm that there is only one working controller on the scene by showing the surroundings(please note that the confirmation of control and acrobatic performance should be one take, if any cuts or skips are noted your team will be disqualified)
